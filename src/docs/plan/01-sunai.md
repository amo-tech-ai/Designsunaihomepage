## How Services & Industries Work Together

### Core Principle

Industry defines what matters.
Services define how we solve it.

The wizard's job:
1. Understand the industry
2. Understand the business goals
3. Select the right services automatically
4. Generate a live dashboard that matches the business

---

## Wizard Flow (5 Screens)

### Screen 1 — Industry

**Question:** What industry is your business in?

**Options:**
- Startup
- Agency
- Fashion
- Real Estate
- E-commerce
- Travel
- Professional Services
- Custom

**System Action:**
- Loads industry rules
- Loads recommended services
- Loads industry language
- Loads dashboard templates

---

### Screen 2 — What Are You Building?

**Options:**
- AI Dashboard
- CRM / Sales System
- AI Chat Assistant
- Automation System
- Internal Operations Tool

**System Logic:**
- Filters available services
- Determines dashboard type
- Determines AI agent roles

**Examples:**
- Fashion + Dashboard → Campaign + Content + Event views
- Real Estate + CRM → Leads + Listings + Follow-ups

---

### Screen 3 — Business Goals

**Client Selects Goals:**
- Increase revenue
- Save time with automation
- Improve customer experience
- Scale services
- Launch faster

**Goal-to-Service Mapping:**
- Increase revenue → CRM, Analytics, AI Advisor
- Save time → Automations, AI Agents
- Improve experience → Chatbots, CRM, Content AI

---

### Screen 4 — Recommended Services

System shows personalized recommendations based on:
- Industry
- Build type
- Goals

**Example (Fashion Brand):**
- AI Dashboard (campaign performance)
- Content & Media AI
- CRM for partners & vendors
- Automation for approvals & scheduling

Each service includes:
- What it does
- Why it matters for their industry
- Core vs Advanced toggle

---

### Screen 5 — AI Level & Scope

**Client Chooses:**
- Core AI (assistive, guided)
- Advanced AI (autonomous, proactive)

**System Adjusts:**
- Number of AI agents
- Automation depth
- Dashboard intelligence
- Timeline & pricing tier

## Client Dashboard V3 Structure

### Entry Transition

**Badge:** Personalized for [Company Name]
**Title:** Your Project Is Ready
**Subtitle:** This dashboard shows what we're building and how AI will support your business.

---

### Section 1: Project Snapshot

**Cards:**
- System Type (dynamic based on selections)
- Industry (from wizard input)
- Primary Goal (from business outcomes)
- Project Size (from complexity)

**Helper Text:**
This is a living project — details can be refined anytime.

---

### Section 2: What AI Is Doing For You

**Title:** How AI Will Help Your Business
**Subtitle:** These AI capabilities are included in your system.

**Capability Cards (dynamic based on selections):**
- AI Helpers Working for You
- AI That Thinks With You
- AI That Creates Content
- Automated Background Work
- AI Knowledge & Intelligence

Each card shows:
- Title
- Description
- 3 key bullets

---

### Section 3: What Happens Next

**4-Step Timeline:**
1. AI finalizes your project plan (complete)
2. Key features and workflows are prepared (in progress)
3. Your team reviews and approves (upcoming)
4. Build or execution begins (upcoming)

**Action Buttons:**
- View Full Project Details (primary)
- Talk to AI About This Project (secondary)

---

### Right Sidebar: AI Project Assistant

**Card 1 — Suggested Next Step:**
Dynamic based on project type:
- Chatbot → Define top 5 customer questions
- Automation → List 3 most time-consuming tasks
- Strategy → Share current strategy docs
- Default → Schedule kickoff call

**Card 2 — Be Aware Of:**
Dynamic risk flags:
- Multiple systems → Extended timelines
- Creative AI → Need brand guidelines
- AI Agents → Need clear boundaries
- Default → Need good data organization

**Card 3 — Helpful Tip:**
Industry-specific guidance:
- Fashion/Luxury → Maintain exclusivity while scaling
- Startups → Start with one high-value feature
- Automation → Best automations are invisible
- Default → Treat AI like a team member

**Quick Action:**
Ask AI a Question button
