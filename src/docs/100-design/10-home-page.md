# SunAI Agency — Home Page Plan

## Overview
Premium AI agency home page inspired by Superside's editorial approach, adapted to showcase AI transformation services with calm luxury aesthetic.

**Target Audience:** Enterprise decision-makers, C-suite executives, innovation leaders  
**Goal:** Position SunAI as the trusted AI transformation partner for luxury brands and enterprise clients  
**Feel:** Confident · Strategic · Editorial · High-end creative studio meets AI intelligence

---

## Page Structure (Vertical Flow)

### Section Order
1. Hero — Above the Fold Impact
2. Trust Bar — Logo Carousel
3. Services Overview — Three Core Pillars
4. Impact Stats — Data-Driven Authority
5. Featured Case Study — Visual Storytelling
6. AI Capabilities — Innovation Showcase
7. Process Overview — How We Work
8. Portfolio Grid — Project Gallery
9. Testimonials — Social Proof
10. CTA Section — Ready to Transform
11. Footer — Comprehensive Navigation

## Design System Application

### Color Usage Summary
- **Authority Green (#1A3A32):** Headlines, CTAs, navigation active states, dark sections
- **Innovation Lime (#C5F467):** Primary CTAs, AI badges, stat highlights, accents
- **Warm Ivory (#FAF8F5):** Main background, light sections
- **Charcoal (#1F2421):** Body text, headlines on light backgrounds
- **Pure White (#FFFFFF):** Cards, elevated surfaces

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Headlines:** 64px → 48px → 36px Editorial Serif
- **Body:** 18px → 16px → 14px Humanist Sans
- **UI Text:** 14px → 12px Sans (labels, badges)

### Spacing Rhythm
- **Sections:** 128px → 96px vertical padding
- **Between sections:** 96px gaps
- **Cards:** 32px internal padding
- **Grid gaps:** 24px

### Glassmorphism Applications
- Hero glass cards (0.7 opacity, 40px blur)
- Navigation header on scroll (0.9 opacity, 20px blur)
- Service cards (optional glass treatment)
- Modal overlays

### Motion Guidelines
- **Entrance animations:** Fade + Slide Up (400ms)
- **Hover effects:** Lift 4px, shadow increase (200ms)
- **Page transitions:** Smooth crossfade (300ms)
- **Stagger delays:** 100ms–150ms between elements
