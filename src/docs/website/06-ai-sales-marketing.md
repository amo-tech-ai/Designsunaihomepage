## FAQ Section

**Q1: Will AI replace our sales team?**
A: No. AI handles repetitive tasks (scoring, follow-ups, scheduling) so your team focuses on closing deals and relationships.

**Q2: How accurate is lead scoring?**
A: 85-92% accuracy after training on your data. Improves over time with feedback.

**Q3: Can AI write in our brand voice?**
A: Yes. We train GPT-4 on your existing content, tone guidelines, and example emails.

**Q4: How long until we see results?**
A: Most clients see 20-30% improvement in qualified leads within 4 weeks.

**Q5: What about data privacy?**
A: GDPR/CCPA compliant. Encryption at rest and in transit. Data never leaves your approved systems.

**Q6: Do you integrate with our existing tools?**
A: Yes. We integrate with major CRMs, email platforms, calendars, and custom APIs.

**Q7: What if leads don't respond to AI emails?**
A: AI monitors engagement. If multiple emails get no response, it stops sending and marks lead as cold.

**Q8: Can we review AI-generated content before it sends?**
A: Absolutely. We offer approval workflows, or fully automated (your choice).
