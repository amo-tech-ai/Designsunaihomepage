## FAQ Section

**Q1: Do I need a lot of data to train AI?**
A: Depends on the task. GPT-4 works with minimal data. Custom models need 1,000+ examples. We assess during discovery.

**Q2: How long until I see results?**
A: Proof-of-concept in 2-4 weeks. Production deployment in 6-12 weeks depending on complexity.

**Q3: What if the AI doesn't perform well?**
A: We benchmark accuracy during prototyping. If we can't achieve target metrics, we recommend alternative solutions or refund.

**Q4: Can you work with our existing tech stack?**
A: Yes. We integrate with any system that has an API or database connection.

**Q5: Do you handle compliance (GDPR, HIPAA)?**
A: Yes. We implement privacy controls, data encryption, and audit trails for regulated industries.

**Q6: What's the difference between GPT-4 and custom models?**
A: GPT-4 is general-purpose and fast to deploy. Custom models are trained on your data for higher accuracy on specific tasks.

**Q7: Do you provide ongoing support?**
A: Yes. All projects include 30 days of support. Ongoing maintenance available for $2.5k-$8k/month.

**Q8: Can AI replace my entire team?**
A: AI augments teams, rarely replaces. Expect 40-80% automation of repetitive tasks, allowing humans to focus on high-value work.
