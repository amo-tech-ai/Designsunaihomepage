## 6 Automotive Use Cases

### 1. Vehicle Finder & Inventory Search
- **Challenge:** 10,000+ vehicles, buyers overwhelmed
- **Solution:** AI asks questions, recommends matches
- **Flow:**
  - New or used?
  - Vehicle type (sedan, SUV, truck)?
  - Budget range?
  - Key features (AWD, sunroof, towing)?
  - Trade-in?
- **Result:** Shows 3-5 perfect matches with photos, specs, pricing
- **Conversion:** 38% request test drive (vs 2.8% site avg)

### 2. Test Drive Scheduler
- **Challenge:** Phone scheduling during business hours only
- **Solution:** AI books test drives 24/7
- **Workflow:**
  - Select vehicle
  - Choose date/time
  - Provide license info
  - Book with specific sales rep
  - Confirmation SMS + calendar invite
  - Reminder 1 day before
- **Result:** 3.2x more test drives booked
- **After-Hours:** 45% of bookings happen 6pm-8am

### 3. Trade-In Valuation
- **Challenge:** Customers don't know trade-in value
- **Solution:** AI provides instant estimate
- **Data Collected:**
  - Year, make, model, trim
  - Mileage
  - Condition (excellent/good/fair)
  - Accident history
  - Photos (optional)
- **Integration:** KBB, NADA, Black Book APIs
- **Result:** 68% of trade-in leads convert to test drive

### 4. Financing & Payment Calculator
- **Challenge:** "Can I afford this car?"
- **Solution:** AI calculates monthly payments
- **Inputs:**
  - Vehicle price
  - Down payment
  - Credit score range
  - Loan term (36/48/60/72 months)
  - Trade-in value
- **Output:** Monthly payment, total interest, APR estimate
- **Result:** 52% of users who see calculator visit dealership

### 5. Service Appointment Booking
- **Challenge:** Service dept. phone always busy
- **Solution:** AI schedules oil changes, repairs, recalls
- **Service Types:** Oil change, tire rotation, brake service, inspection, recall
- **Workflow:** Select service â†’ Choose date â†’ Provide VIN â†’ Book â†’ Confirmation
- **Result:** 280 service appointments/month (was 120)

### 6. Lead Follow-Up & Nurture
- **Challenge:** 80% of car buyers research 6+ months
- **Solution:** AI nurtures until ready to buy
- **Nurture Actions:**
  - New inventory alerts matching preferences
  - Price drop notifications
  - Incentive/rebate updates
  - Financing deals
  - Trade-in value checks
- **Result:** 28% of nurtured leads buy within 90 days
