### Budget (30 points)
- Budget allocated: +30
- Budget range confirmed: +25
- Budget under review: +15
- No budget: +0

### Authority (25 points)
- C-level/VP: +25
- Director/Manager: +20
- Individual contributor: +10
- Unclear: +5

### Need (25 points)
- Urgent pain point: +25
- Active search: +20
- Exploring options: +15
- Curious: +10

### Timeline (20 points)
- 0-30 days: +20
- 31-90 days: +15
- 91-180 days: +10
- 180+ days: +5
