## FAQ for E-Commerce

**Q1: Can it recommend products from my inventory?**
A: Yes. Integrates with your product catalog, filters by stock, price, category.

**Q2: How does it handle size recommendations?**
A: Learns from your size chart, product measurements, and customer reviews to suggest best fit.

**Q3: What about returns and exchanges?**
A: Chatbot can initiate return process, print labels, or hand off to support agent.

**Q4: Can it work on mobile?**
A: Absolutely. Optimized for mobile shoppers (70% of e-commerce traffic).

**Q5: Does it support multiple languages?**
A: Yes. Auto-detects and responds in 95+ languages (Enterprise tier).

**Q6: How do abandoned cart emails work?**
A: Integrates with Klaviyo/Omnisend or uses built-in email system. Fully customizable templates.

**Q7: Can we A/B test chatbot messages?**
A: Yes. Test different greeting messages, discount amounts, timing for cart recovery.

**Q8: What's the setup process?**
A: 1) Install widget, 2) Connect product catalog, 3) Train on FAQs, 4) Customize flows, 5) Go live. 4-6 weeks total.
