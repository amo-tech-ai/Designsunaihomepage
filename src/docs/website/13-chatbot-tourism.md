## 6 Tourism Use Cases

### 1. Destination Recommender
- **Challenge:** Travelers don't know where to go
- **Solution:** AI suggests based on preferences
- **Questions:**
  - Travel style (adventure, relaxation, culture)?
  - Budget per person?
  - Travel dates?
  - Group size?
  - Interests (beaches, hiking, food, history)?
  - Climate preference?
- **Result:** Recommends 3-5 destinations with photos, highlights
- **Conversion:** 42% request detailed itinerary (vs 4% site avg)

### 2. Tour & Activity Booking
- **Challenge:** Visitors browse but don't book
- **Solution:** AI guides through booking process
- **Workflow:**
  - Show available tours
  - Check availability for dates
  - Answer questions (duration, difficulty, included)
  - Add to cart
  - Process payment
  - Send confirmation + tickets
- **Result:** 58% booking completion (was 18%)

### 3. Hotel & Accommodation Help
- **Challenge:** Travelers need lodging advice
- **Solution:** AI recommends hotels, resorts, Airbnb
- **Recommendations:**
  - Based on budget
  - Neighborhood/location
  - Amenities (pool, gym, breakfast)
  - Reviews/ratings
  - Distance to attractions
- **Action:** Direct booking or hand off to reservation system
- **Result:** 35% book accommodation through chatbot

### 4. Itinerary Builder
- **Challenge:** Planning multi-day trips is overwhelming
- **Solution:** AI creates custom day-by-day itinerary
- **Includes:**
  - Daily activities/tours
  - Restaurant recommendations
  - Transportation between locations
  - Time allocations
  - Weather considerations
  - Budget breakdown
- **Format:** Downloadable PDF, shareable link
- **Result:** 68% of itinerary users book at least one tour

### 5. Travel FAQs & Support
- **Challenge:** "What's the weather?" "Need a visa?" "Best time to visit?"
- **Solution:** AI answers 24/7
- **Topics:**
  - Visa requirements
  - Weather/best season
  - Currency/tipping
  - Transportation options
  - Safety/health advisories
  - COVID protocols
  - Packing lists
- **Result:** 73% questions resolved without agent

### 6. Post-Booking Engagement
- **Challenge:** Travelers forget about booking
- **Solution:** AI sends reminders and tips
- **Engagement Sequence:**
  - Booking confirmation
  - 30 days before: Packing list
  - 14 days: Local tips, weather forecast
  - 7 days: Transportation info
  - 1 day: Itinerary reminder
  - After trip: Review request, next destination ideas
- **Result:** 4.6/5 avg rating, 25% rebook within 12 months

## FAQ for Tourism

**Q1: Can it handle multi-day itineraries?**
A: Yes. AI creates day-by-day plans with activities, dining, transport, timing.

**Q2: Does it support multiple languages?**
A: 95+ languages with auto-detection. Responds in user's language automatically.

**Q3: Can travelers pay directly in chat?**
A: Yes. Secure payment via Stripe/PayPal. PCI compliant.

**Q4: What about group bookings?**
A: Handles groups up to 50. Collects names, dietary restrictions, special requests.

**Q5: Can it recommend restaurants?**
A: Yes. Integrates with Yelp, Google Maps, TripAdvisor for recommendations.

**Q6: What if weather cancels a tour?**
A: Chatbot can send cancellation notices, offer rebooking, or process refunds.

**Q7: Does it work with Airbnb/Booking.com?**
A: Via API integrations. Can show availability and deep-link to booking.

**Q8: How do we update tour availability?**
A: Syncs with your booking system (Rezdy, FareHarbor, etc.) in real-time.
