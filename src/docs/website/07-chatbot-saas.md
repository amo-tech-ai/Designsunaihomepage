## FAQ for SaaS

**Q1: How does the chatbot learn our product?**
A: We train it on your docs, help center, support tickets, and product videos. You can update the knowledge base anytime.

**Q2: Can it integrate with our existing help desk?**
A: Yes. Seamless handoff to Intercom, Zendesk, Freshdesk with full conversation history.

**Q3: What about multi-language support?**
A: GPT-4 supports 95+ languages. Auto-detects user language and responds accordingly.

**Q4: Can we customize for different user tiers?**
A: Absolutely. Different responses for free vs. paid users, admins vs. members.

**Q5: How do we prevent wrong answers?**
A: Chatbot cites sources from your docs. You can review conversations and flag incorrect responses.

**Q6: What if users prefer human support?**
A: Users can always request human handoff. Bot gathers context first to save agent time.

**Q7: Can it send proactive messages?**
A: Yes. Triggered by events: signup, feature use, inactivity, usage limits, etc.

**Q8: How do we measure ROI?**
A: Dashboard tracks activation rate, ticket deflection, upgrade conversion, churn reduction, and cost savings.
