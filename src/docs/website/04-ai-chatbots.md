## FAQ Section

**Q1: How accurate is the chatbot?**
A: GPT-4 provides 85-95% accuracy when trained on your knowledge base. Improves over time with feedback.

**Q2: Can it handle complex questions?**
A: Yes. For complex issues, the bot gathers information then escalates to a human agent with full context.

**Q3: How long until it's live?**
A: Starter chatbots: 6 weeks. Professional: 8 weeks. Enterprise: 10-12 weeks.

**Q4: What if it gives wrong information?**
A: Chatbot cites sources. You can review conversations, correct responses, and retrain the bot.

**Q5: Does it work in multiple languages?**
A: Yes. GPT-4 supports 95+ languages. Auto-detects and responds in user's language.

**Q6: Can we customize the personality?**
A: Absolutely. We train the bot on your brand voice, tone guidelines, and example conversations.

**Q7: How does human handoff work?**
A: Seamless. User clicks "Talk to human" or bot detects escalation need. Full conversation history transfers to agent.

**Q8: What about data privacy?**
A: All conversations encrypted. GDPR/CCPA compliant. Data stored in your region. Option for self-hosted.
